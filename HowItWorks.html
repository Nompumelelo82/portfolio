How StudentStay Works

Finding your perfect student home is simple with our 3-step process

1

Create Your Account

Sign up in minutes with your student details. Tell us about yourself, your preferences, and what you're looking for.

  • Quick Registration: Just your student number, name, email, and preferences
  • Set Your Preferences: NSFAS funding status, room type (single/double), budget
  • Verify Your Email: Secure your account and start browsing immediately
2

Browse & Filter

Search through hundreds of verified properties and find exactly what you need with our powerful filters.

  • Smart Filters: Price range, location, NSFAS-friendly, accredited, distance to campus
  • View Photos: See high-quality images of rooms, facilities, and common areas
  • Check Transport: View available transport routes and distance to your campus
  • Save Favorites: Bookmark properties you like and compare them later
3

Connect & Move In

Chat with landlords, schedule viewings, and secure your new home - all through our platform.

  • Instant Chat: Message landlords directly and get quick responses
  • Schedule Viewings: Book a time to visit and see the property in person
  • Ask Questions: Get all the info you need about lease terms, deposits, and amenities
  • Secure Your Spot: Once you're happy, finalize the agreement and move in!

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to find accommodation?

Most students find their perfect place within 5-7 days. With our instant chat feature, you can connect with landlords immediately and speed up the process!

Is StudentStay free to use?

Yes! Creating an account, browsing properties, and messaging landlords is completely free for students. We want to make finding accommodation as easy as possible.

Are all properties NSFAS-friendly?

Not all, but you can easily filter to show only NSFAS-friendly accommodation. We clearly mark which properties accept NSFAS funding to save you time.

How are properties verified?

We verify each landlord's identity and visit properties to ensure they meet safety standards. We also check accreditation status with universities and local authorities.
